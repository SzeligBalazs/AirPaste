About AirPaste


AirPaste helps you to transfer text between your devices. The application makes it quick and easy. A WebSocket server runs in the background and the client(s) listen on the other devices. I’m developing it with Flutter and I’m planning to publish to Android and Windows platforms.


Sending mode


In sending mode you can send text-based
messages to the connected devices.






Receive mode


In receive mode you can receive text-based
messages from the connected devices.
